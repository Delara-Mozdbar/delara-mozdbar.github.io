Case Study

Nubaa Health —
unified medical
booking platform

A B2B2C platform connecting patients to doctors, dentists, labs, pharmacies, and home care services — designed across three separate apps with a team of 7, from kickoff to delivery in 7 months.

my role — 4 hats worn

  • 01 Lead UX designer — user flows, wireframes, information architecture, usability testing
  • 02 UI contributor — admin panel design and iteration
  • 03 Client liaison — ran 13 review sessions directly with the client from July–December 2021
  • 04 Informal PM — coordinated 4 developers and 2 UI designers across delivery phases
Timeline August 2021 – March 2022
Team 7 people — me + 2 UI designers + 4 developers
Platforms Patient app · Provider app · Admin portal
Languages English, Arabic, Kurdish (RTL support)

Outcome 01

All major UX decisions approved across 13 client review sessions — zero full redesign cycles after prototype stage

Outcome 02

12+ service categories — doctors, dentists, labs, ambulance, pharmacy, veterinarian, home care — organized under one coherent home screen

Outcome 03

Provider app shipped with offline capability — designed for low-connectivity clinical environments in Kurdistan
